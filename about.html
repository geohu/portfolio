About Me

Designing with empathy,
shipping with precision.

I'm George Hu, a Principal Product Designer with over 16 years of experience across consumer and enterprise product applications. My design philosophy is rooted in my early career in startups — where iterative learning, rapid shipping, and wearing multiple hats were foundational to building great products and high-performing teams.

As an individual contributor, I thrive across the full spectrum of product development — from navigating the ambiguity of early alignment to obsessing over the pixel-level detail of interaction behavior. My breadth of skill allows me to move quickly through product and organizational complexity, while creating the space and clarity for teams to do their best work together.

In today's era of AI and tools like Claude Code, the ability to create and ship has reached a new velocity. With a strong foundation in both craft and code, I'm developing new methods to direct and shape output with greater speed and precision — delivering clearer, more intentional experiences to end users.
